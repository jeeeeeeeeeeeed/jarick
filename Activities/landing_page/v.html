Hoan Kiem Lake

Hoan Kiem Lake, also known as the "Lake of the Returned Sword," is a captivating urban escape. Imagine strolling along lush green pathways, surrounded by pristine waters, picturesque bridges, and ancient pagodas. The scenery is a masterpiece of tranquility amidst the urban hustle.

Thien Mu Pagoda

This pagoda has a deep history dating back to the 17th century. As you explore its grounds, you'll uncover stories and legends that have shaped the culture and heritage of Hue.
